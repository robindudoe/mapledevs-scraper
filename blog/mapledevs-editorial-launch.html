Introducing The Maple Feed

MapleDevs Editorial • April 22, 2026

Welcome to the first edition of The Maple Feed. This is more than just a blog, it’s a commitment to the Canadian game development community. Every week, our editorial team will bring you the most important news from studios across the country, from the bustling hubs of Montreal and Vancouver to the rising scenes in Alberta and the Maritimes.

Our Mission

We built MapleDevs to cut through the noise of US-centric job boards. The Maple Feed is the next step in that journey. We want to provide you with the context you need to build your career in Canada, whether you’re a junior looking for your first role or a lead developer navigating the changing landscape of major studio acquisitions.

What to Expect

Moving forward, you can expect weekly roundup articles, deep dives into studio culture, and tips for landing roles in the Canadian market. Everything is curated to be relevant to you, the developer working in the Great White North.
