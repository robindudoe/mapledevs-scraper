Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Privacy First Approach

MapleDevs is built with a privacy-first mindset. We do not use third-party tracking scripts like Google Analytics, Datadog RUM, or PostHog. Your browsing behavior remains yours. We only collect data that is strictly necessary to provide our service.

2. Information We Collect

Job Seekers: We do not require an account to browse jobs. If you choose to create a profile or sign up for alerts, we store your email and the professional details you voluntarily provide. This data is never sold or shared with advertisers.

Employers: We collect studio contact information and job details to verify and publish your listings. Payment information for featured roles is never stored on our servers as all transactions occur via manual Interac e-Transfer.

3. Automated Data Collection

We aggregate job listings from official studio career pages. This information is publicly available and we process it to provide a centralized hub for Canadian developers. If you are a studio owner and wish to have your listings removed or updated, please contact us at mapledevsbusiness@gmail.com.

4. Studio Verification & Trust

MapleDevs is committed to maintaining a high-trust environment. Every studio listed on our platform is manually verified by our team to ensure they have a legitimate operational presence in Canada. We do not allow 'ghost jobs', unpaid roles (excluding internships), or deceptive listings. We regularly audit our database to ensure all links and details remain accurate and fresh.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data or unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time. We are a Canadian-owned platform and comply with relevant privacy standards to protect your information.
